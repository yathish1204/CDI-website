About Us

Founded in 1994, Contract Decor International is the hospitality industry's source for high-quality, custom-manufactured window treatments and bedcovers.

We offer complete measuring, manufacturing, and installation services for draperies, sheers, top treatments, hardware, shades, blinds, bedspreads, dust ruffles, and endless accessories. All are custom-made to size and available in a variety of styles. Let the experienced craftspeople in our upholstery division reupholster your furniture with the option of pickup and delivery or having the work done onsite.

Working with Contract Decor International is easy. You can build your specifications, submit jobs and track projects online — or simply call us on the phone. Our family-run, hands-on approach means each job is handled personally by one of the principals in the company.

Windows and bedcovers are the first things customers see when they walk in the room. That's your chance to make a great first impression. That's also the window of opportunity for us — to earn your trust, and your future business, with great follow through.
